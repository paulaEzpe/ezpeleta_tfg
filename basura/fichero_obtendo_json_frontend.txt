Abstract: In this paper we prove that the defocusing, cubic nonlinear S chr¨ odinger initial value
problem is globally well-posed and scattering for u0∈L2(R2). The proof uses the bilinear esti-
mates of [67] and a frequency localized interaction Morawet z estimate similar to the high frequency
estimate of [25] and especially the low frequency estimate o f [28].
